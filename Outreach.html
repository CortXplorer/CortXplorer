Zeiss Colloquium Innovation Talk

Check out the talks in German

Max Happel was invited to speak in this colloquium on how internal factors - such as motivation and attention - and external environmental conditions - such as sufficient exercise - contribute to a "healthy learning environment." 

"ZEISS Colloquium Innovation Talk" is dedicated to current topics in science. The ZEISS Colloquium offers external scientists from various fields a platform to present their research in a one-hour "Innovation Talk" followed by a discussion.
